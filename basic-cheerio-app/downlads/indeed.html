Job Description: Sales Intern

Casetabs is looking for a Sales “warrior” intern with who is ready to crush sales goals. In this role, you will have the opportunity to bring disruptive technology to the way surgeries are coordinated across the US. Think “The Uber of Surgeries.” This position is located in Santa Monica, CA.

About the company (casetabs.com)

  • Casetabs mission is to coordinate the world’s surgeries so doctors and nurses can focus on patients.
  • Digital HealthTech company improving surgery coordination with innovative web and mobile technology.
  • Well Capitalized - Series A funding completed.
  • Majority Owner- Evolution Financial Group which is an integrated suite of businesses spanning nearly 500 employees and ten global offices (www.evofg.com).

What we're looking for:

  • Fun, energetic and a fearless sales warrior;
  • Ability to maintain and prioritize a large pipeline in Salesforce;
  • Conduct product demonstrations via Gotomeeting or in-person;
  • Quickly and thoroughly learn Casetabs’ product, the industry and the industry challenges;
  • Desire to travel (Up to 10-20% of the time);
  • A team player with excellent communication skills;
  • Outgoing, creative, optimistic, and has a solution focused attitude;
  • Comfortable engaging in constructive debate- confident and competitive;
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment;
  • Desire to work for a startup tech company.

Requirements:

  • Strong networking and time management skills (LinkedIn + Salesforce).
  • Excellent computer skills including knowledge of MS Word, PowerPoint and Excel.

.

Ideally you'll also have experience with:

  • Sales and Marketing;
  • Extreme comfort in cold-calling (70-100 calls/day).

Work environment

  • Water Garden office complex in Santa Monica (sofas, fire-pit, B-ball, football, corn hole, foosball etc.).
  • Flexible office hours (manage your business, time zone sets business hours).
  • Enthusiastic, creative, supportive, laser- like focus.
  • Attire (Dress for what makes you productive AND respectful of those around you).

Compensation

  • Competitive pay (Base + commission).

Job Type: Full-time

Salary: $15.00 /hour

Required education:

  • Associate
